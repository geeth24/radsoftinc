MyPanchang Hindu Panchangam At A Glance


MyPanchang displays the two essential things in the Hindu Panchangam. First, it shows the Tithi. Then it shows the Nakshatra. However, the best part of the app is viewing the Panchangam just from your home screen using widgets! Currently, MyPanchang is the only app that displays your live Panchanga right from your home screen with Widgets.
